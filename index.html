About Me

I build scalable AI systems and solve complex problems through machine learning and data science, with a focus on NLP, computer vision, and time series analysis. I enjoy turning research ideas into end-to-end ML solutions that create real-world impact.

I hold a BSc in Mathematics (Honours) from Hacettepe University and an MSc in Data Science from Università degli Studi di Padova. I’ve worked as an AI Intern at FOREO, where I developed multimodal translation systems and scalable backend services, gaining hands-on experience with distributed systems and production ML pipelines.

I’m a native Turkish speaker, fluent in English, conversational in Italian, and currently learning Spanish. My technical work centers around Python, using PyTorch, TensorFlow, and Hugging Face Transformers for deep learning and multimodal models, and FastAPI, Scikit-learn, and XGBoost for building production-ready ML pipelines.

In addition, I’m interested in applying AI to sustainability-focused challenges, particularly in renewable energy and resource optimization. Outside of work, I enjoy photography and travel, drawing inspiration from different cultures and perspectives.
